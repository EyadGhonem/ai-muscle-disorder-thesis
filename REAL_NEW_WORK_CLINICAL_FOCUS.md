## **2. ✅ CLINICAL INTERPRETATION FRAMEWORK**
**Real New Work:** Medical significance mapping of radiomics features

**Key Clinical Insights Discovered:**
- **Minimum Intensity** (6.32% importance): Indicates darkest tissue regions - **fat infiltration patterns**
- **Mean Intensity** (5.75% importance): Average echogenicity - **muscle vs fat composition**
- **GLCM Dissimilarity** (5.63% importance): Local intensity differences - **muscle fiber organization**
- **Equivalent Diameter** (4.90% importance): Muscle cross-sectional area - **atrophy assessment**

**Clinical Value:**
- Each feature now has **direct medical meaning**
- Doctors can **understand model decisions**
- Enables **clinical validation** of AI recommendations
